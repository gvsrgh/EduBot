ABOUT THE INSTITUTE
=================================================================

ESTABLISHMENT & AFFILIATION:
- Founded: 1998
- Sponsored by: Siddhartha Academy of General and Technical Education
- Approved by: All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE)
- Affiliated to: Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK)
- Status: Autonomous Institution
- Website: pvpsiddhartha.ac.in

CAMPUS LOCATION:
- Address: Kanuru, Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, India
- Campus Area: 19.98 acres

ACCREDITATION & RECOGNITION:
- NAAC Grade: A+ (National Assessment and Accreditation Council)
- NBA Accreditation: All undergraduate programmes accredited by National Board of Accreditation
- Education Approach: Outcomes-based education aligned with industrial requirements
- Environment: 100% ragging-free campus
- Recognition: Known for academic excellence and strong employability focus
