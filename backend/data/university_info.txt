Prasad V. Potluri Siddhartha Institute of Technology (PVPSIT) established in 1998 and sponsored by the Siddhartha Academy of General and Technical Education. ([pvpsiddhartha.ac.in][1])

The institute is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education and is permanently affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada. ([pvpsiddhartha.ac.in][1])

PVPSIT has autonomous status and offers outcomes based education aligned with industrial requirements. ([pvpsiddhartha.ac.in][2])

The campus is located in Kanuru, Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, India, and covers an area of approximately 19.98 acres. ([pvpsiddhartha.ac.in][3])

The institute is accredited with an A+ grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council and all undergraduate programmes are accredited by the National Board of Accreditation. ([pvpsiddhartha.ac.in][1])

The academic programmes include undergraduate Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) courses in disciplines such as Computer Science and Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Electrical & Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Data Science and Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning. ([pvpsiddhartha.ac.in][4])

Postgraduate programmes include Master of Technology (M.Tech) with specialisations including Machine Design and Microwave & Communication Engineering, and a full-time Master of Business Administration (MBA) programme. ([pvpsiddhartha.ac.in][4])

Admission to undergraduate engineering programmes is based on state-level entrance examinations such as AP EAMCET and ECET, postgraduate engineering admissions use GATE and AP PGECET, and MBA admissions use AP ICET. ([pvpsiddhartha.ac.in][5])

B.Tech course fees are INR 70,000 per year under Convenor Quota and INR 2,10,000 per year under Management Quota. MBA programme fees are approximately INR 1.2 lakhs for the full duration and M.Tech fees are up to around INR 1.8 lakhs for the full duration. ([Collegedunia][6])

Hostel facilities are available with rooms accommodating 2-4 students and monthly costs around INR 1,500 per student; girls' hostel is on/near campus and boys' hostel is located off campus. ([Collegedunia][7])

The institute offers modern infrastructure including a central library, laboratories, auditorium, gymnasium, sports facilities, cafeteria and campus-wide Wi-Fi connectivity. ([Careers360][8])

Placement records show a high percentage of students placed with packages ranging generally from around INR 3 LPA to higher offers, with some reports citing highest packages up to around INR 44 LPA and average packages around INR 3.5-4.5 LPA; top recruiters include companies such as TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture and others. ([Shiksha][9])

The institute provides a curriculum and syllabus structured to achieve defined program outcomes and educational objectives, prepared and approved by its Boards of Studies and Academic Council. ([pvpsiddhartha.ac.in][10])

Contact details include the principal Dr. K. Sivaji Babu and administrative office phone numbers and email for general enquiries. ([pvpsiddhartha.ac.in][11])

PVPSIT is recognized for academic excellence, a 100% ragging-free environment and a strong focus on employability through its training and placement cell. ([LinkedIn][12])
